fortuitous

adj.偶然的

1. spotlight

n. a focus of public attention

n. a lamp that produces a strong beam of light to illuminate a restricted area; used to focus attention of a stage performer

2. dim

adj. lacking in light; not bright or harsh

​ If the future of something is dim, you have no reason to feel hopeful or positive about it. 悲观的

e.g. She stood waiting in the dim light. 

  The prospects for a peaceful solution are dim.

v. become or make darker

e.g. Their memory of what happened has dimmed.

​ Their economic prospects have dimmed.
